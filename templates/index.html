How to Get Started

For Teachers

  1. Create an Account: Click the Register button and fill in your details. Select "Teacher" as your role.
  2. Verify Email: Confirm your email address through the verification link sent to your inbox.
  3. Add Questions: Create a comprehensive question bank by adding questions across different chapters, difficulty levels, and question types.
  4. Create Tests: Design tests for your students based on specific learning objectives or curriculum requirements.
  5. Monitor Progress: Track student performance through detailed analytics and performance reports.
Register as a Teacher

For Students

  1. Create an Account: Click the Register button and fill in your details. Select "Student" as your role.
  2. Select Your Teacher: Choose your teacher from the dropdown list during registration.
  3. Take Assigned Tests: Complete tests assigned by your teacher from your dashboard.
  4. Create Custom Tests: Generate your own practice tests based on chapters and difficulty levels you want to focus on.
  5. Use Personalized Tests: Let our AI system recommend questions based on your performance history.
